Who we are

Serious about the work.
Easy to work with.

At Praxis, we work collaboratively with clients to make their most difficult problems seem simple.

Since 1999, Praxis has built a reputation for integrity, honest advice, and a genuine concern for the success of our clients — from Fortune 500 companies to governments and community organizations. We are apolitical, independent, and objective: a third party you can put in front of any board, council, or community.

We have no sales staff and no account representatives. The seasoned specialists who scope your engagement are the ones who deliver it — people with real backgrounds in business, government, and the institutional sectors we serve.
